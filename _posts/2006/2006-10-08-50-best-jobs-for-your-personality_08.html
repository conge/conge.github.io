Match Your Personality to the Right Career

To find work satisfaction and success, job seekers use this essential reference to match their personality to the right career. 50 Best Jobs for Your Personality covers 300 jobs for the six major “Holland” personality types and provides more than 140 “best jobs” lists.


Divided into five parts, this book helps readers develop a better understanding of their personality with an easy-to-use assessment for learning which jobs fit them best. Next, the lists show readers the best jobs for each personality type, organized by earnings, growth, education level, and more. Then they can study in-depth descriptions for the best jobs in each personality group, which include information such as earnings, projected growth, education and training required, job duties, skills, and much more.
